Citrem Plus

This is the best medicine used to treat fever and allergic problems. The ingredients consist of Levocetirizine Dihydro chloride, Phenylephrine HCL, Ambroxol HCl and Paracetamol.This medication is used for nasal congestion, allergic rhinitis and fever with a cold. For asthmatic patient, this medication is recommended.It can also be combined with an antibiotic when there is the severe bacterial infection of the respiratory tract.
